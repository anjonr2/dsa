One's compliment of a binary number is obtained by flipping all the bits
For example, the one's compliment of 13 (binary 1101) is : 

0000 1101 (binary of 13)
1111 0010 (one's compliment of 13)

Two's compliment 

The two's complement is obtained by taking the one's complement of a number and then adding 1. For example, the two's compliment of 13 binary (1101) is :
